# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Warden a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background or identity. We act in ways that keep the
community open, welcoming, and respectful.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal or political attacks,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct reasonably considered
inappropriate.

## Security note

This is a security project. Do **not** post working exploits or undisclosed
vulnerabilities in public issues or discussions — follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for responsible disclosure.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove, edit, or
reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Report abusive
behavior to the maintainers via the contact in the repository.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
